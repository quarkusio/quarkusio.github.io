Quarkus Releases

Release history and support status for all Quarkus versions.

Recommended for production: use the latest LTS release, Quarkus 3.33. LTS releases receive critical fixes and security patches for 12 months.

New minor versions of Quarkus are released every 4–6 weeks. LTS releases are published every 6 months and maintained for 12 months with critical bug fixes and security patches. Non-LTS releases are supported until the next minor release.

Running an unsupported version? See our End-of-Life guidance.
