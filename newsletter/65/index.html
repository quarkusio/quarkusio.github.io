Enterprise support options for Quarkus

The Quarkus community just got stronger: alongside the long‑standing Red Hat Build of Quarkus, IBM has announced the IBM Enterprise Build of Quarkus. Organizations now have a choice between two enterprise distributions built on the same community core — a clear sign of the project's growth and maturity.
