About the Commonhaus Open Source Support Initiative

Quarkus is a member of the Commonhaus Foundation, which runs the Open Source Support Initiative (OSSI) to help connect organizations with qualified commercial support providers.

OSSI partners are vetted by the Foundation and must meet requirements around security practices, CVE remediation experience, and responsible disclosure. Participation in the initiative does not grant governance rights or authority over the Quarkus project roadmap.

Learn more about Commonhaus Foundation OSSI.
